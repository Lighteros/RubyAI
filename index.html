About

RUBYAI is a platform for open-source products built using artificial intelligence technology. Our service provides a unique opportunity to use all the most in-demand neural networks in one place at a fair price.

Any developer will be able to monetize their AI model, which will be deployed inside our server and run on Nvidia T4 GPU.
